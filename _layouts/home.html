Features

Verified Users

Unlike other apps and platforms, WeConnect uses a verification process to determine which accounts are valid.

Information relevant to our Jamaican society

Each community hub has access to information on Jamaica's missing persons, wanted persons and general news.

Forums

Users can view and create posts on forums. Each community has a forum, and users can also create their own forums.

Alerts

Our app generates alerts specific to each community hub. These may include water and power outage notices, general news etc.

Have some questions?

What do I need to sign up?

You need an email address and a picture ID. Ensure that the name on your ID is the name you use to sign up.

What happens to my ID and camera photos after the verification process?

These images are discarded. We do not store them.

Can I join more than one community forum?

Yes. No further verification is needed.

What if psosts on the app are considered to be harmful or dangerous?

Posts can be reported so that the admin and creator of the post is made aware of this issue. Posts can also be deleted by the aforementioned individuals.
